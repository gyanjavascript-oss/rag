🔌 Plugins

8 intelligence plugins not available in any other DDQ platform.

⚖️ Cross-DDQ Consistency Auditor

Not available elsewhere

Compares one LP session's answers against every other session — finds genuine factual contradictions (strategy, AUM, team, hold period, fees) that would be damaging if LPs compared notes.

🔍 LP Question Pattern + Document Gap Report

Not available elsewhere

Clusters all investor questions by topic, scores documentation coverage per topic, and generates a prioritised document production backlog — telling you exactly what policies and memos to write next.

🧠 Investor-Specific Memory Layer

Not available elsewhere

Analyses all past conversations with an LP to build a preference profile: focus areas, response style, sensitivity topics, jurisdiction, and follow-up patterns. The profile is automatically injected into future answer generation for this investor.

⏱ Staleness Monitor

Not available elsewhere

Reviews all past DDQ answers and flags those likely outdated — specifically answers older than 60 days that contain time-sensitive data such as AUM, IRR, team composition, NAV, or fund strategy.

🏛 SEC/EDGAR Regulatory Filing Sync

Not available elsewhere

Searches public SEC EDGAR for your fund's filings (Form ADV, Form D, Form PF) and cross-references them with your DDQ answers. Flags contradictions between what you've told LPs and what you've reported to the SEC.

🌿 ESG Auto-Population

Not available elsewhere

Scans all uploaded fund documents for ESG content and auto-populates the standard ILPA ESG DDQ sections: policy, responsible investment, exclusions, climate/TCFD, DEI, and reporting. Shows a coverage score and flags gaps.

🌐 Jurisdiction + Regulatory Language Mapping

Not available elsewhere

Detects the LP's jurisdiction and regulatory regime from their entity name and uploaded documents. Provides adapted DDQ response language compliant with local rules (AIFMD, FSA-Japan, MAS-Singapore, SFC-HK, DIFC, etc.).

📬 Proactive LP Update Orchestrator

Not available elsewhere

Identifies investors who received answers that may now be materially inaccurate. For each affected LP, drafts a professional proactive update email — turning a compliance risk into a relationship-building touchpoint.
