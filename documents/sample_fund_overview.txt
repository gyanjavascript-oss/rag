SAMPLE FUND OVERVIEW
====================

Fund Name: Apex Growth Fund III
Fund Type: Closed-End Private Equity Fund
Fund Size: $500 million target, hard cap $600 million
Vintage Year: 2024
Strategy: Growth equity investments in technology and healthcare sectors
Geographic Focus: North America and Western Europe
Investment Stage: Series B through pre-IPO
Typical Check Size: $25 million - $75 million

FEES AND ECONOMICS
==================

Management Fee: 2.0% per annum on committed capital during the investment period,
thereafter 1.5% on invested capital (net of realizations).

Carried Interest: 20% carried interest with an 8% preferred return (hurdle rate).

European Waterfall: The fund uses a European-style waterfall where LPs must receive
return of all capital contributions plus the preferred return before the GP receives any carry.

Management Fee Offset: 100% of transaction fees, monitoring fees, and director fees
received by the GP or its affiliates will be applied to offset management fees.

FUND TERMS
==========

Fund Life: 10 years from the final close, with two 1-year extensions at GP discretion
(subject to LPAC approval for any further extensions).

Investment Period: 5 years from the final close.

GP Commitment: The GP will commit not less than 2% of total fund commitments.

Clawback: Standard clawback provision requiring the GP to return excess carry
distributions if aggregate distributions to LPs fall short of the preferred return
on a whole-fund basis.

KEY PERSON PROVISIONS
=====================

The fund designates [Partner A] and [Partner B] as Key Persons. A Key Person Event
occurs if both Key Persons cease to devote substantially all of their professional
time to the fund. Upon a Key Person Event, the investment period is automatically
suspended until LPAC approves its reinstatement by majority vote.

LP ADVISORY COMMITTEE (LPAC)
=============================

The LPAC consists of up to 7 LP representatives. LPAC approval is required for:
- Related party transactions
- Material conflicts of interest
- Extension of the investment period or fund life beyond permitted extensions
- Amendments to the LPA requiring greater than majority approval

GOVERNANCE AND REPORTING
========================

Quarterly Reports: LPs receive quarterly financial statements within 60 days of
quarter-end, including portfolio company updates, valuation summaries, and capital
account statements.

Annual Audited Financials: Audited annual financial statements prepared in accordance
with GAAP are delivered within 120 days of fiscal year-end.

Annual Meeting: The GP holds an annual meeting for LPs.

INVESTMENT STRATEGY
===================

The fund targets companies with proven business models generating $10 million or more
in annual revenue, with clear paths to profitability or already EBITDA-positive.

The fund does not make venture-stage or seed investments. Target sectors include:
enterprise software, health technology, digital infrastructure, and fintech.

ESG POLICY
==========

The fund has a formal ESG policy. All investments undergo ESG due diligence as part
of the standard investment process. The GP is a signatory to the UN Principles for
Responsible Investment (UNPRI). An annual ESG report is provided to LPs.

VALUATION POLICY
================

Portfolio company valuations are prepared quarterly in accordance with the IPEV
Valuation Guidelines and ASC 820 (Fair Value Measurement). Valuations are reviewed
by an independent third-party valuation firm annually and upon significant events.

TRANSFERS AND SECONDARY SALES
==============================

LP interests may not be transferred without GP consent, which shall not be unreasonably
withheld for transfers to affiliates. The GP maintains a right of first refusal on
secondary sales to third parties.

DISTRIBUTIONS
=============

Distributions are made at GP discretion, subject to maintaining adequate reserves.
The fund targets distributing proceeds from realizations within 30 days of receipt.
Tax distributions may be made to cover estimated tax obligations arising from
fund income.
